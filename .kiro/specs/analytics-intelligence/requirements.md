# Analytics & Business Intelligence Spec

## Introduction

Provide comprehensive analytics and business intelligence features to help photographers and media professionals understand their work patterns, client preferences, and business performance.

## Glossary

- **Business Intelligence**: Data analysis tools for business decision-making
- **Performance Metrics**: Quantitative measures of photography and business success
- **Trend Analysis**: Identification of patterns over time in photography work
- **Client Analytics**: Insights into client behavior and preferences

## Requirements

### Requirement 1: Photography Performance Analytics

**User Story:** As a professional photographer, I want detailed analytics about my photography performance so I can improve my skills and understand what works best.

#### Acceptance Criteria

1. WHEN analyzing my portfolio THEN the system SHALL provide technical quality metrics across all photos
2. WHEN reviewing client selections THEN the system SHALL identify which photo characteristics clients prefer most
3. WHEN tracking improvement THEN the system SHALL show skill progression over time with visual charts
4. WHEN comparing equipment THEN the system SHALL analyze performance differences between cameras and lenses
5. WHEN planning shoots THEN the system SHALL suggest optimal settings based on historical success patterns

### Requirement 2: Business Performance Dashboard

**User Story:** As a photography business owner, I want comprehensive business analytics so I can make data-driven decisions about pricing, services, and growth strategies.

#### Acceptance Criteria

1. WHEN viewing business metrics THEN the system SHALL display revenue, client satisfaction, and project completion rates
2. WHEN analyzing client behavior THEN the system SHALL show selection patterns, preferences, and engagement metrics
3. WHEN tracking efficiency THEN the system SHALL measure time from shoot to delivery and identify bottlenecks
4. WHEN forecasting THEN the system SHALL predict busy periods and resource needs based on historical data
5. WHEN optimizing pricing THEN the system SHALL analyze which services and packages perform best financially
